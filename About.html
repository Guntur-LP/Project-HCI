About JCC

Introducion

Jockey's Conference Center (JCC) is a renowned conference center located in Jakarta, Indonesia. It has a long-standing history as one of the earliest and most popular convention centers in the city. JCC was first opened in 1974 and has since hosted numerous significant national and international conferences, exhibitions, fairs, indoor sports events, and musical concerts.

Over the years, JCC has been the venue for notable events such as the 11th Non-Aligned Movement Conference and the Asian-African Conference. With its extensive experience and reputation, JCC continues to attract a wide range of events throughout the year, making it a preferred choice for various gatherings and activities.

facilities

The facilities at the Jockey's Conference Center are well-equipped to meet the diverse needs of event organizers. The center features a spacious main lobby and an atmospheric lower lobby, along with a round theater-style hall called the Plenary Hall. Additionally, there are two exhibition halls, Exhibition Hall A and B, an Assembly Hall, a banqueting hall known as the Cendrawasih Room, and several meeting rooms that can be configured to accommodate specific event requirements. The business center available at JCC provides support for meetings, and the bonded warehouse enables fast customs clearance for temporarily imported goods.

Conveniently located in Jakarta, Jockey's Conference Center benefits from its proximity to an independent 6,000-space car park situated right in front of the convention center. Moreover, there are several mid- to high-range hotels within walking or short driving distance, offering convenience for event attendees and organizers a like.

Certification

Jockey's Conference Center (JCC) is proud to announce that we have passed an in-depth set of assessments to achieve the honor of ISO 22000:2018 certification. The implementation of ISO 22000:2018 allows us to constantly improve our quality management systems and the processes of our organization. This certification shows that we are truly committed to the quality of our ingredients, as part of our business culture and philosophy. JCC is dedicated to providing a high standard of service and ensuring the safety and quality of the food served at our facility.
